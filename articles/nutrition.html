Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? We've Got Answers.

Find answers to the most common questions about our nutrition guides, learning resources, and educational content. If you still need help, feel free to contact us.

01 Are these nutrition guides suitable for beginners?

Yes. Every nutrition guide is written in simple, beginner-friendly language with step-by-step explanations. Whether you're starting your fitness journey or expanding your nutrition knowledge, our guides are designed for all experience levels.

02 Are your nutrition guides based on scientific evidence?

Yes. Our articles are based on scientific research, established nutrition principles, and practical fitness experience to provide reliable and easy-to-understand educational content.

03 How often do you publish new nutrition articles?

We regularly publish new nutrition guides and update existing articles so our educational content stays accurate, relevant, and aligned with the latest fitness knowledge.

04 Can these guides help with muscle gain and fat loss?

Absolutely. Our nutrition resources explain calorie intake, protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, meal planning, and recovery strategies to support muscle building, fat loss, and overall health.

05 Do I need supplements to follow these nutrition guides?

No. We encourage building a strong nutritional foundation through balanced eating and healthy lifestyle habits first. Supplements are discussed only when appropriate and should never replace a nutritious diet.

06 Are the fitness calculators free to use?

Yes. Every fitness calculator on Atul Tomar Fitness is completely free and designed to help you estimate calories, protein intake, body fat percentage, hydration needs, and other important health metrics.
